Creamy Cottage Cheese Scrambled Eggs

Description

This dish is a proteïn-rich twist on the widely known breakfast staple. Easy and quick to make with only 4 ingrediënts, this is the ideal breakfast for athletes and everyone else.

ingrediënts

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 tsp chopped chives
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs until smooth.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet.
  3. Add the beaten eggs, let cook undisturbed until the bottom of the eggs begins to firm.
  4. Stir cottage cheese and chives into the eggs.
  5. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set.
  6. Season to taste.
