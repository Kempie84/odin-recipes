Dutch Bacon Pancakes

Description

This is a Dutch classic. Pancakes with Bacon and Syrup. Perfect for a Sunday breakfast or make it for dinner like the Dutch so.

IngrediÃ«nts

  • 500 grams flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 800 grams milk
  • pinch of salt
  • smoked bacon
  • Dutch pancake syrup, for topping

Steps

  1. Combine the flour and half of the milk.
  2. Stir with a whisk untill all the milk is absorbed.
  3. Add the eggs and whisk until combined.
  4. Whisk in the rest of the milk and add salt to taste.
  5. Melt a knub of butter in the skillet.
  6. Cover the bottom of the pan with the pancake mixture.
  7. Quickly add 2 strips of bacon on top of the pancake.
  8. Flip the pancake and bake 1 min on the other side.
  9. Serve the pancake with the Syrup.
